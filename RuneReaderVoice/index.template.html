Life like voice synthesis for World of Warcraft - every NPC, narrator, book and quest brought to life.

* * *

RuneReader Voice is a companion desktop application for the RuneReader Voice WoW addon. Together they intercept NPC dialog, quest text, and narration as you play and synthesize it into natural-sounding speech in near realtime - using either a local AI voice model or a self-hosted remote TTS server.

Voices are created from reference samples, giving each NPC a consistent, character-appropriate sound across every session - you just play the game.

Local & remote processing Auto-updates 494+ voice samples Quest, Books & NPC dialog Player name voicing with or without titles
